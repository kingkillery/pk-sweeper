## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `6f72b74cecf8d1fec5c147ae932161bf0f37a68b` still defaults TUI delivery to off, still moves normal sends into `waiting` immediately after `sendChat`, and still relies on chat events to populate `activeChatRunId` and clear the run. That matches the reported stuck-spinner / `no active run` failure mode rather than proving a fix landed.
