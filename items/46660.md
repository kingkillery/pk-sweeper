## Summary

Current `main` does not implement the proposed A2A turn hook. The plugin hook registry still lacks `agent_to_agent_turn`, the hook runner exposes no `runAgentToAgentTurn`, and the A2A flow still runs ping-pong turns plus the final announce without dispatching any per-turn plugin event.
