## Summary

Current `main` contains related Codex ACP auth-handling and ACP permission-troubleshooting work, but I did not find strong evidence that issue #63702's specific `session/new` -> `Internal error` failure is resolved well enough to close. The report is recent, from a non-maintainer, and the repo evidence points to plausible causes rather than a verified fix for this exact path.
