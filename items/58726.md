## Summary

Current main does not satisfy the issue for repeated identical `session_status` calls. The loop detector can block critical known polling/no-progress and ping-pong loops, but the generic same-tool/same-args detector is still explicitly warning-only, and `session_status` is not classified as a known poll tool.
