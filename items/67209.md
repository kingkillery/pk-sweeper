## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still shows the unfixed logging-config path, while this PR's approach introduces unresolved hot-path IO and config-pipeline regressions, so it is neither implemented on `main` nor safe to auto-close.

## Evidence

  - file: [src/logging/config.ts:14](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/7875092f4ddc6f2dbe218bea8506c9ac1da87c39/src/logging/config.ts#L14)

Replacing `readLoggingConfig()` with `fs.readFileSync` + `JSON5.parse` would therefore put blocking IO/parsing on a hot path, matching the unresolved P1 review concern.
