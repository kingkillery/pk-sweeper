## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement this PR's Unicode tokenizer fix: MMR tokenization in `extensions/memory-core/src/memory/mmr.ts` still extracts only ASCII tokens plus special-case CJK, and FTS tokenization in `extensions/memory-core/src/memory/hybrid.ts` and `extensions/memory-core/src/memory/manager-search.ts` still omits combining marks and NFC normalization. The reported non-English failures remain plausible and directly reproducible from the shipped regexes.

## Evidence

- **MMR still drops non-ASCII words outside CJK:** `extensions/memory-core/src/memory/mmr.ts:48` lowers the text, then `extensions/memory-core/src/memory/mmr.ts:50` matches only `/[a-z0-9_]+/g`, with separate CJK handling at `extensions/memory-core/src/memory/mmr.ts:37` and `extensions/memory-core/src/memory/mmr.ts:52-70`. That means Finnish/Arabic/Hebrew/etc. do not become MMR tokens on current `main`.
