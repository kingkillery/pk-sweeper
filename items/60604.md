## Summary

Keep #60604 open. Current main does not implement the PR's Telegram per-message sequential key behavior, and the observable steer-mode delivery problem is not fully solved on main. The related discussion shows this PR may be incomplete or needs rework, but there is not enough high-confidence evidence to close it as implemented or superseded.
