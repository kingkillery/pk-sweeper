## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has partial mitigations for the reported loop, but the exact issue is not conclusively resolved end-to-end: the hard-reset fallback still gives the same generic `reserveTokensFloor` advice the report called misleading, and retry/bootstrap reinjection concerns are only partially mitigated through newer config and continuation-skip behavior rather than clearly eliminated for this path.
