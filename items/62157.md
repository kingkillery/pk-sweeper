## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still intentionally drops all non-error final payloads after a successful block-streamed turn, so the underlying bug is not implemented on `main`. The proposed PR also looks incomplete: replacing that branch with `hasSentPayload()` alone would miss coalesced-stream cases where streamed chunks were merged into one outbound block but the final reply remains split across multiple payloads, causing duplicates.
