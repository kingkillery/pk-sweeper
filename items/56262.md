## Summary

Keep open. Current main shows that bundled Mistral is intentionally routed through `api: "openai-completions"`, and the repo history says a Mistral 422 fix shipped in `2026.3.28`, so the original diagnosis in #56262 looks at least partly obsolete. But the issue now also has a newer April 20 report describing 404 failures on versioned Mistral models in `2026.4.15`, and this read-only review did not produce strong enough evidence that all remaining Mistral failures are resolved on current main.
