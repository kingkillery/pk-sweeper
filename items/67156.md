## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still ships `skills/canvas/SKILL.md` without YAML frontmatter, and the bundled skill loader drops skills that do not provide a frontmatter description. The PR still fixes a real, unfixed main-branch defect. The prior review discussion was useful: the optional `metadata.openclaw.os` suggestion was correctly called out as too restrictive for non-mac gateways, and the submitted PR history reflects that follow-up.
