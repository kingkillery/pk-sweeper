## Summary

Keep open: the reported behavior still matches current main. `summarizeChunks()` still iterates over chunks and wraps each `generateSummary()` call in `retryAsync({ attempts: 3 })`, and I found no circuit-breaker state, cooldown, or consecutive-failure stop condition in the current `src/agents/compaction.ts` implementation.
