## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still places Gmail watcher startup on the awaited gateway sidecar path, while model-pricing remains asynchronous. The issue discussion already isolated the real blocker to Gmail watcher, and recent Apr 24 commits/tests on `main` explicitly made waiting for startup sidecars the default behavior rather than fixing it.
