## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still intentionally reuses a single `runId` across retry/fallback, keeps the run in processing until fallback completes, and allows a large outer retry budget. I did not find a strong OpenClaw-side fix on `main` that specifically addresses the reported single-turn retry churn/stuck-processing behavior well enough to close conservatively.
