## Summary

Current `main` has strong evidence that the `channels list` hot-path regression was addressed, plus newer cron responsiveness coverage and help/version bootstrap bypasses. But this issue bundles three command trees (`channels`, `cron`, `message`), and I could not verify end-to-end on a built current-main CLI in this checkout. Conservatively, that is not strong enough to auto-close.
