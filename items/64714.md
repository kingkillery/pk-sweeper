## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still hard-rejects `streamTo` whenever `runtime !== "acp"`, and the test suite still asserts that rejection for `runtime: "subagent"`. The reported strict-provider autofill failure is therefore not implemented away on `main` at `fd65caf4b0bad1b0ac27a82d349c22e92167e7bc`.

## Evidence

  - file: [src/agents/tools/sessions-spawn-tool.ts:198](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/fd65caf4b0bad1b0ac27a82d349c22e92167e7bc/src/agents/tools/sessions-spawn-tool.ts#L198)
