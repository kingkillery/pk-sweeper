## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still hardcodes QQ Bot media SSRF hostnames and does not expose a `channels.qqbot.mediaAllowHosts` config surface. The request is recent and specific enough to stay actionable. One caveat: the reporter’s example hostname `multimedia.nt.qq.com.cn` appears to already match the shipped `*.qq.com.cn` allowlist, so that concrete failure may have had another cause, but the requested configurability is still not implemented.
