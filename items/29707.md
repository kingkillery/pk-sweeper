## Summary

Keep open. Current main still excludes Amazon Nova/non-Anthropic Bedrock models from prompt-cache retention, and tests/docs explicitly preserve that behavior. The linked #20815/#20866 path appears to have addressed Nova cachePoint failures by disabling cache retention for non-Anthropic Bedrock models, not by adding Nova-native caching support requested here.
