## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still requires `plugins.entries.<id>.hooks.allowConversationAccess=true` for non-bundled `llm_input`/`llm_output`/`agent_end` hooks, and the native plugin manifest type/loader do not define or read a manifest-level `permissions.conversationAccess` field. This RFC is coherent, current, and not implemented on `main`.

## Evidence

- **Runtime still enforces config-only conversation access gate:** `src/plugins/registry.ts` blocks non-bundled conversation hooks unless `policy?.allowConversationAccess === true`, and the diagnostic text explicitly points plugin authors to `plugins.entries.<id>.hooks.allowConversationAccess=true`.
  - file: [src/plugins/registry.ts:1331](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/ec8dbc4595583816f71af2568df61dbe7c090e1f/src/plugins/registry.ts#L1331)
