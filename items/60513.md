## Summary

Current main does not contain the PR's requested fix. The audit check still accepts skill realpaths anywhere inside the workspace root, while the loader enforces containment under the resolved skills directory/root, so the reported mismatch remains. The PR is small, related to open issue #49408, and appears to contain useful remaining work rather than being obsolete or superseded.
