## Summary

Keep #30828 open. Current main has related rate-limit handling after a provider error occurs, including auth-profile rotation/cooldowns, model-scoped cooldown metadata, and a Retry-After cap to avoid SDK stalls. It does not implement the requested proactive/user-configurable per-model tokens-per-interval or requests-per-interval limiter, and the OpenAI header-respect idea in the issue comment remains broader than the current Retry-After handling.
