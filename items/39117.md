## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the PR's queued busy receipt, aborted-reply suppression, or richer `sessions.reset` audit metadata, and the existing active-run lifecycle on `main` supports the outstanding review concern that force-clearing a busy session too early could make a still-streaming run look idle.
