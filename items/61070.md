## Summary

Current `main` does not expose the requested subagent operating-principles/failure-attempt config or the requested proactive compaction threshold/message-retention config. The linked PR also appears to have been blocked by unrelated config-surface problems rather than this narrower feature being rejected outright, so there is not strong evidence to close conservatively.
