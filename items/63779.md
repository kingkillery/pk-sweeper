## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still routes successful ACP completion through a mirrored `assistant` transcript entry (`model: "delivery-mirror"`) and explicitly does not queue a heartbeat/system-event wake for that success path, so a parent that ended with `sessions_yield` is not guaranteed to resume from the ACP completion alone.

## Evidence

  - file: [docs/concepts/session-tool.md:87](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/86f8c826e20a01d1bba67c0ba6e73182fbf0455f/docs/concepts/session-tool.md#L87)
