## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still documents Mattermost channel/group support and still routes non-DM Mattermost posts through the websocket monitor path, but I found no code/history evidence that this specific regression was fixed on `main` after the report. The available tests cover mention gating and channel-kind mapping, not the reported end-to-end websocket delivery failure for public/group channel posts, so there is not enough evidence to close conservatively.
