## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not include the proposed macOS `sandbox-exec` wrapper or `sandboxProfile` exec option, and the documented isolation model still relies on configured sandbox backends plus VM guidance for macOS. This draft PR is recent and still represents a real core/runtime change that needs maintainer product/security judgment rather than conservative auto-close.
