## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` clearly added fast paths for `status`, `gateway status`, and root `--help`, which likely mitigates part of the report, but the issue is broader and the reported `openclaw help` path still appears to go through the normal Commander/preAction bootstrap. Without a Windows repro on this checkout, and with a related open issue cross-referenced, there is not strong enough evidence to auto-close conservatively.

## Evidence

  - file: [src/cli/command-catalog.ts:42](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/86f8c826e20a01d1bba67c0ba6e73182fbf0455f/src/cli/command-catalog.ts#L42)
