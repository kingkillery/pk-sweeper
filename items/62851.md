## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still ships and recommends `arcee/trinity-large-thinking` as the default Arcee model, the embedded runner still distinguishes `main` and `subagent` lanes, and the only relevant recent hardening I found is a generic embedded-runner/webchat `surface_error` improvement rather than an Arcee-specific fix, guard, or regression proof. I could not find evidence on current `main` that this exact main-session vs subagent mismatch is resolved well enough for a conservative close.
