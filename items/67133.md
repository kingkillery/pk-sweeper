## Summary

Current `main` does not match the reported behavior: cron jobs record `lastRunAtMs` and run history, while the cited `hooks.internal.entries.heartbeat` / `self-improvement` hooks are not bundled OpenClaw internal hooks on `main` and heartbeat is configured through the agent heartbeat system instead.
