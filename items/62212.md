## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` advertises `google-vertex` ADC support, but the Google plugin still handles `google-vertex` through a hook alias and the transport still turns non-JSON credentials into `x-goog-api-key` headers. I did not find strong evidence on `main` that the reported ADC 401 path has been fixed, and the issue is cross-referenced to an open PR (#65023).
