## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the proposed `before_response_emit` hook or an equivalent run-scoped response-mutation/blocking path. The plugin hook surface still lacks that hook, the hook runner has no `runBeforeResponseEmit`, and the embedded runner only emits observe-only `llm_output` before returning the final response.
