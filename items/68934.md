## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still lacks the proposed `signal-polyfill` alias in the A2UI rolldown config, and the checked-in `a2ui.bundle.js` still contains two separate `signal-polyfill` reactive cores (`$1`-suffixed and unsuffixed). The PR is therefore addressing a live bug, not an already-landed or obsolete change.
