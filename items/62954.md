## Summary

Keep open. Static inspection of current `main` still shows the reported path: heartbeat runs know they are heartbeats, but session-usage persistence still unconditionally writes `model`/`modelProvider`, and `resolveSessionModelRef` still prefers those persisted runtime fields for UI/current-model display.
