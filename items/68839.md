## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still unconditionally deletes `FOLLOWUP_QUEUES` by key in the drain `finally`, and the surrounding queue lifecycle makes the reported stale-drain/orphaned-replacement race plausible. I did not find an equivalent fix or regression test on `main`; the PR's identity guard and added test still look materially useful.
