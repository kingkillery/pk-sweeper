## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still constructs raw subagent internal-event envelopes, prepends them to the inbound agent prompt, persists that prompt as a `user` transcript turn with `inter_session` provenance, and forwards live chat/history payloads to Control UI without stripping internal runtime-context blocks.
