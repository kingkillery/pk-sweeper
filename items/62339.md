## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still lets WhatsApp heartbeat recipient resolution union pairing-store entries with configured `allowFrom`, even though the DM `allowlist` access path excludes pairing-store approvals. That leaves the reported unauthorized cron/heartbeat send path plausible. The related `#20108` hardening improved outbound target enforcement, but it does not close this specific gap.
