## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still skips session-source reconciliation on `session-start`/`watch` unless a full reindex, targeted sync, force run, or in-process dirty flag is present, so missed transcript events can remain unindexed after restarts or other event-bus drops. The related status-count mismatch for archived session transcripts also still exists on `main`.

## Evidence

- **Session sync gate still excludes clean `session-start` and `watch` runs:** `shouldSyncSessionsForReindex` returns `false` for `reason === "session-start" || reason === "watch"` unless force/targeted/full-reindex applies, and otherwise depends on transient `sessionsDirty` state.

The session listener subscribes to future `onSessionTranscriptUpdate` events only; there is no startup enumeration of session files vs indexed rows here.
