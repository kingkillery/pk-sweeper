## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` partially improved hook context by exposing `runId`, `modelProviderId`, and `modelId`, but the reported core behavior is still present: `before_model_resolve` is invoked inside each `runWithModelFallback` candidate attempt, and plugins still do not receive `attempt` or `isFallbackRetry` to distinguish retries.

## Evidence
