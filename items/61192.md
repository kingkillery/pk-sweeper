## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not support the issue's specific root-cause hypothesis that Telegram `senderName` is being forwarded into an OpenAI-compatible message `name` field, but there is also no landed fix or payload-level reproduction proof that would justify closing the bug. The underlying 400/cooldown failure remains plausible and needs the outbound request body the maintainer asked for.
