## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still turns Slack non-recoverable auth failures into a rejected channel task, and the gateway channel manager still auto-restarts exited channel tasks. That matches the reported Slack-enabled restart-loop shape closely enough that conservative cleanup should not close this yet.

## Evidence
