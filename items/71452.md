## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still hardcodes a 25-item display cap in the shared message formatter, and the MS Teams `list-pins` / `channel-list` action handlers do not pass through a caller `limit` or expose cursor-style pagination. The issue body slightly overstates the Teams backend gap: current `main` already follows Graph `@odata.nextLink` for pins and channel lists, so the remaining work is mainly user-controlled limits/pagination and renderer support.
