## Summary

Current `main` already contains targeted fixes for the three symptoms in #56270: WhatsApp reconnect-loop handling, self-reply/echo suppression, and Codex/OpenAI final-answer delivery behavior in chat surfaces.

## Evidence

- **WhatsApp reconnect loop is explicitly stopped for conflict closes:** `isNonRetryableWebCloseStatus` treats WhatsApp close status `440` as non-retryable, and the monitor stops with relink guidance instead of continuing the reconnect loop.
  - file: [extensions/whatsapp/src/auto-reply/monitor.ts:40](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/7d1891e6e63dc35dd9dcb688ba308049e68fe695/extensions/whatsapp/src/auto-reply/monitor.ts#L40)

- **Reconnect behavior is covered by regression tests:** The WhatsApp web auto-reply monitor test suite asserts that status `440` stops without retrying and that a reconnected listener gets a fresh watchdog window.
  - file: [extensions/whatsapp/src/auto-reply.web-auto-reply.connection-and-logging.e2e.test.ts:133](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/7d1891e6e63dc35dd9dcb688ba308049e68fe695/extensions/whatsapp/src/auto-reply.web-auto-reply.connection-and-logging.e2e.test.ts#L133)
