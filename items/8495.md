## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` enforces WhatsApp group admission/sender gating, but it does not implement the requested cleanup behaviors: no `autoLeaveUnauthorized` config, no WhatsApp group-join event/webhook handling, and no WhatsApp `leaveGroup` message action exposure.

## Evidence
