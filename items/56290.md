## Summary

Current `main` still appears susceptible to the reported recursion. `createConfigIO()` still defaults its logger to `console`, missing-env warnings still call `logger.warn`, patched `console.warn` still routes through `getLoggerLazy()`, and `getLogger()` still re-enters `loadConfig()` without a matching reentrancy guard. The only added guard/test on `main` is for re-entrant console-settings resolution, which does not cover the issue’s `console.warn -> getLogger -> loadConfig` path.
