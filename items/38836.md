## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still treats `SIGTERM` as a stop, not a restart-with-drain, and the restart notification machinery is scoped to one sentinel session or to subagent recovery rather than all interrupted Feishu/channel sessions.

## Evidence

- **SIGTERM still skips restart drain:** `src/cli/gateway-cli/run-loop.ts:139`-`225` sets `isRestart` only for the SIGUSR1 path. The drain logic (`markGatewayDraining()`, `waitForActiveTasks()`, `waitForActiveEmbeddedRuns()`) runs only inside `if (isRestart)`, while `onSigterm` calls `request("stop", "SIGTERM")` at `src/cli/gateway-cli/run-loop.ts:222`-`225`.
  - file: [src/cli/gateway-cli/run-loop.ts:139](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/d3dd61f2c5bd6cea175544f3a76a68ec11fa17c2/src/cli/gateway-cli/run-loop.ts#L139)

- **Docs still describe SIGTERM as stop-only:** `docs/cli/gateway.md:42`-`43` documents `SIGUSR1` as the restart signal and says `SIGINT`/`SIGTERM` handlers stop the gateway process.
  - file: [docs/cli/gateway.md:42](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/d3dd61f2c5bd6cea175544f3a76a68ec11fa17c2/docs/cli/gateway.md#L42)

  - file: [src/gateway/server-close.ts:161](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/d3dd61f2c5bd6cea175544f3a76a68ec11fa17c2/src/gateway/server-close.ts#L161)

Startup consumes that single sentinel in `src/gateway/server-restart-sentinel.ts:307`-`415`; there is no scan over all recently interrupted sessions.
