## Summary

Current `main` does not implement this PR, and the surrounding code/tests still depend on ensure-sourced ACP identities with stable IDs staying `pending` until later reconciliation. The proposed change would also make ensure identities authoritative during merges, which creates a plausible stale-ID regression before status refresh. This is not strong evidence for closure; the item should stay open for revision or maintainer judgment.
