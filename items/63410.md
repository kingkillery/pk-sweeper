## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still treats `web.heartbeatSeconds` as a local liveness/logging/watchdog timer, and I found no OpenClaw-side protocol keepalive change that would clearly address a recurring WhatsApp 408 idle-disconnect loop. The related cross-referenced WhatsApp issues are also still open.

## Evidence

- **`web.heartbeatSeconds` drives logging/watchdog, not WhatsApp traffic:** `monitor.ts` resolves `heartbeatSeconds` into the connection controller, and the heartbeat callback only logs connection stats / stale-message warnings; it does not send a presence update, IQ stanza, or other application-level keepalive.
