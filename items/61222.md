## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has both Telegram update dedupe and dispatch-level inbound dedupe, but the report is not strongly disproven: `dispatch-from-config` still releases inbound dedupe on the error path, and the later cross-reference to #69303 describes a replay vector that matches this class of duplicate `message_id` symptoms after failures.
