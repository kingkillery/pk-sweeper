## Summary

Current `main` does not implement per-job cron cache-control overrides. Cron job types, protocol schema, and docs expose `lightContext` and `toolsAllow`, but no `disableCache` or per-job `contextPruning` field, and the embedded runner still reads cache-pruning only from global `agents.defaults.contextPruning`.
