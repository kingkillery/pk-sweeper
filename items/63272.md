## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has ClawHub skill `search`/`install`/`update`, but no `skills uninstall` path in the CLI, gateway methods, or public docs. The proposal is coherent, and the lockfile-backed update flow means the reported orphaned-lockfile/reinstall behavior is still a real gap rather than an obsolete request.
