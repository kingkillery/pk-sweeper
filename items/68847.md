## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `938b53698ec17934a5d1a6cffb279c584f64d9f3` still has the reported `nodeWakeById` leak path, the latest release `v2026.4.23` still ships it, and the report is recent and concrete rather than stale or incoherent.
