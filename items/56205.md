## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` already routes workspace-scoped `write` through atomic post-write verification on both POSIX and Windows, but that is not strong evidence that the reported false-positive `Write failed` user-facing error is fixed end-to-end. The chat/tool-error layer still preserves mutating tool failures until a later matching success, and the report has no concrete failing payload/log to prove the original symptom is obsolete.
