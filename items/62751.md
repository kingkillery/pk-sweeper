## Summary

Current `main` still limits `session:patch` hook emission to the gateway `sessions.patch` RPC path. Both `/model` and the `session_status(model=...)` tool mutate the session entry directly with `applyModelOverrideToSessionEntry(...)` plus `updateSessionStore(...)` and do not trigger internal hooks, so the reported gap remains open rather than being conservatively closable.

## Evidence
