## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still does not implement `google-vertex` as a manifest-owned/setup-owned provider, and the Vertex-specific endpoint/onboarding pieces described in PR #62103 are not present elsewhere on `main`. The PR remains materially useful rather than already-implemented or obsolete.
