## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still does not implement the specific Baileys reliability hooks requested in #7433. OpenClaw has partial group/LID handling, but there is no `getMessage` callback, no `cachedGroupMetadata` socket option, and no proactive group/LID update listeners that would justify a conservative close.

## Evidence
