## Summary

Current `main` already decouples Discord `MESSAGE_CREATE` intake from long-running reply generation. `DiscordMessageListener.handle()` now fire-and-forgets the handler instead of awaiting it, while actual per-session work is queued in the inbound worker with separate timeout controls and coverage proving immediate return/non-serialization behavior.

## Evidence

- **Listener no longer blocks on handler completion:** `DiscordMessageListener.handle()` calls `this.onEvent?.()` and then dispatches `this.handler(data, client)` via `Promise.resolve().then(...)` without awaiting it. The inline comment explicitly says the listener now hands work off without blocking Carbon and leaves ordering/timeouts to the inbound worker queue.
