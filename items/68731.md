## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `938b53698ec17934a5d1a6cffb279c584f64d9f3` still limits BlueBubbles contact-name enrichment to group chats, the resolver remains phone-only and explicitly skips email handles, and the issue has recent maintainer engagement asking for a PR rather than signaling closure.
