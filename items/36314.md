## Summary

Current `main` has adjacent robustness work (global unhandled-error handling, channel health-monitor restarts with restart caps, diagnostics/stability bundles, and config auto-recovery), but it does not clearly implement the issue’s requested built-in gateway-process watchdog, degraded-startup mode, or the proposed richer gateway-wide recovery behavior well enough to close conservatively.
