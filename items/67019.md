## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` makes GLM a supported `zai` path and Cloudflare AI Gateway an Anthropic-only path, so the issue's reported routing chain is contradictory. There is also current-main evidence of Z.AI-specific transport handling and a live `glm-4.7` assistant-text test, but I could not reproduce or disprove the reported garbled-output/session-corruption behavior without credentials. That is not strong enough for an auto-close.
