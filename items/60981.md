## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain the PR's claimed PathGuard surface: filesystem policy is still `workspaceOnly`-only, `sessions_spawn` has no filesystem tightening input, generated config/schema/help do not expose allow/deny or subagent FS ceiling settings, and media/apply_patch paths on `main` are not wired to a canonical allow/deny PathGuard layer. The PR discussion also shows unresolved P1 correctness/security concerns in the proposed implementation.
