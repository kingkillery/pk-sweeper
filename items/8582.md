## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` appears to have implemented the ElevenLabs Voice Call streaming STT support and added schema/documentation coverage, but the duplicate-plugin-warning complaint remains unresolved: duplicate plugin ids are still warned on, docs explicitly say duplicate drops are logged, and I found no suppress/override flag for intentional overrides.
