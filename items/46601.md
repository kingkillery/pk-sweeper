## Summary

Keep open. Current main still routes Gemini CLI audio understanding through `read_many_files` text/file handling, and the generic CLI runner only has image attachment hydration for Gemini `@path` flows. I found no current-main evidence of real multimodal audio attachment support for headless Gemini CLI prompts.
