## Summary

Current `main` still knows the media-understanding provider/model internally, but the inbound body shown to the assistant is still formatted as `[Image]/[Audio]/[Video]` plus `Description` or `Transcript` only. The requested `Analysis model:`-style telemetry has not landed in core, and the existing `/status` surfacing is a separate path.

## Evidence
