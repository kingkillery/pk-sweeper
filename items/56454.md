## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still hardcodes the shared image-tool timeout to 30 seconds, the media-understanding runtime still aborts requests on that timeout, and `agents.defaults.imageTimeoutSeconds` does not exist on `main`. I found no evidence that this PR’s behavior is already implemented or otherwise obsolete.
