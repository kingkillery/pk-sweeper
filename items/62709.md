## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has related OpenAI-compatible transport fixes and GPT-5-only empty-turn recovery, but the evidence does not prove that a custom non-GPT provider like `hermes/hermes-agent` is now handled correctly. The report remains plausible, and the repo still documents multiple OpenAI-compatible failure modes that depend on backend payload shape or compat flags.
