## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` partially recognizes `GOOGLE_CLOUD_API_KEY` for `google-vertex` in `resolveEnvApiKey()`, but the broader request is not implemented: the key is still missing from provider secret/env-var registries, shell-env fallback discovery, and the public provider docs.

## Evidence

  - file: [src/agents/model-auth-env.ts:27](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/ec8dbc4595583816f71af2568df61dbe7c090e1f/src/agents/model-auth-env.ts#L27)

That means `GOOGLE_CLOUD_API_KEY` is still not part of the known secret-scrubbing list on main.
