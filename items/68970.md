## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has a TUI-specific reconnect/backfill gap relative to Web UI: the TUI reloads history once after reconnect and after some `chat.final` events, but it ignores `session.message` transcript updates and does not recover on event gaps beyond showing status text. That matches the reported post-restart symptom closely enough that this is not safe to auto-close.

## Evidence
