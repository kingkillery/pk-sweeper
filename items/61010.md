## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still allows filesystem access outside the workspace by default; the codebase documents that the workspace is only the default cwd, with actual isolation requiring opt-in sandboxing and/or `tools.fs.workspaceOnly=true`. This means the reported concern is not implemented away on `main`, even though the issue body lacks a strong reproduction.
