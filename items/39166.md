## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `ed6094b3014c56882da15a7942130b00ea410c6e` does not implement the PR’s ACP timeout behavior: ACP spawns still do not accept `runTimeoutSeconds`, `sessions_spawn(runtime="acp")` does not forward that field into `spawnAcpDirect`, ACP dispatch still omits the gateway `timeout` param, and there is no `acp.defaultRunTimeoutSeconds` config surface on `main`.
