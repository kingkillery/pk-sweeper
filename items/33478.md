## Summary

Keep open. Current main has partial thread/topic announce routing support, but the requested `sessions_send` structured callback contract is not implemented, and maintainer triage on April 25, 2026 explicitly said this issue should remain open until structured skip/no-reply outcomes plus thread inheritance are covered by the handoff work/tests.
