## Summary

Current `main` still has the filename-contract mismatch for dreaming/backfill: the session-memory hook writes `memory/YYYY-MM-DD-slug.md`, while dreaming ingestion and `memory rem-backfill` still only accept exact `memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md`. Main does contain a partial mitigation for `/new` and `/reset` startup context, but that does not resolve the reported dreaming/backfill gap.

## Evidence
